Demystify Elements

I've had my experience in front-end development; however, I'd like to get a better understanding on when to use some at the right time.

You'll find that there are so many choices, and while many are very firm on when and how to use them, others are really up in the air.

  • <Article>

    Self-contained composition in a document/page. Learn more from MDN.

    This element can be a bit tricky trying to wrap your head around because it behaves like a page with multiple sections, but sometimes it's hard to know when a section of content is stand alone or better as a group.

  • <linear-gradient>

    Creates an image consisting of progressive transition between two or more colors along a straight line. Learn more from MDN

    I really want to get better at using linear-grdient. It's one of those magical elements that can transition wonderful visuals for your content.

  • While not a specific element, I'd like to learn more attributes that can assist elements in certain events, accessibility support, and performance improvement.
