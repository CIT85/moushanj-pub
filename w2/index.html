Why Am I Taking This Course?

Why is Learning HTML Important

Every day, we interact with the web, whether it's through social media, online shopping, or browsing news. Just as it's valuable to understand the workings of daily skills in our physical world, it's worth learning about the web we expose ourselves to so often.

HTML is the foundation of most websites we visit. By understanding it, you gain insight into the digital realm you navigate daily, making your online experiences more informational and fun.
